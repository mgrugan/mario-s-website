CMR 25e Photograph by Mario Grugan

Combining experience in statistics, engineering, and social media growth.

I'm pursuing a Bachelor's in Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University. On campus I serve as social media lead for Carnegie Mellon Racing, a 150-member team building open-wheeled and hybrid racecars. In that role I directed media efforts, created the content, and led a campaign that raised a record-breaking $37,000 for the team.

Outside of school, I manage a network of Instagram theme pages that reach over 15 million followers and generate more than 120 million impressions each month. One example is @ocean.destinations, which has 5.2 million followers. I've been growing and managing Instagram pages for over six years and have helped multiple clients organically scale their presence and gain tens of thousands of followers.

I actively apply what I've learned from my degree to optimize both the revenue and reach of my network — developing predictive models, analyzing performance data, and leveraging insights to fine-tune content strategy. This data-driven approach has significantly increased engagement rates and monetization potential across the entire portfolio.
